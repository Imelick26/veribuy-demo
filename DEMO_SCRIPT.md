>
> Look at what comes back:
>
> **Fuel Injector Leak** — flagged as Critical. This is a known failure pattern across 2021 to 2024 model year EcoBoost engines. The injectors develop micro-cracks at the O-ring seat, and multiple NHTSA complaints cite *fire risk*. Estimated repair: $600 to $1,100.

> **Water Pump Premature Failure** — flagged as High severity. This is actually the most-reported issue on NHTSA for this model year. The internal bearing fails between 18,000 and 35,000 miles. Estimated repair: $900 to $1,400.

> **12-Volt Battery and BCM Defect** — also High. The Body Control Module has widespread reports of parasitic drain, killing the battery in two to three days. Repair can run up to $1,800 if hardware replacement is needed.
